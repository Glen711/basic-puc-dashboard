Cognitive Study Strategies

The 3-Pass Method

For derivations and math. Copy with help step-by-step first. Then attempt with partial peeking. Finally, solve on a completely blank page.

The Plumbing Analogy

For electronics. Visualize Voltage as water pressure, Current as volume of water flow, and Resistors as narrowed pipe diameters.

"Why" Sheets

For chemistry reactions. Avoid blind memorization. Always log down the electrochemical reason *why* an electron shifts or a bond snaps.
